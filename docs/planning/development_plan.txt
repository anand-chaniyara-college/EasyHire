1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

This document outlines the development lifecycle of EasyHire, optimized for a 
specialized 3-member student team. By leveraging parallel workstreams—Backend 
(Anand), DevOps (Krish), and Frontend (Saloni)—the project is designed for 
completion within an 8-week (2-month) academic cycle.
