1. INTRODUCTION
================================================================================

1.1 PURPOSE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This Software Requirements Specification (SRS) document provides a complete and 
comprehensive description of the requirements for the Recruitment and Interview 
Management System (EasyHire). This document is intended for:

    a) Software Developers - To understand functional and technical requirements
    b) Quality Assurance Team - To develop test cases and validation criteria
    c) Project Managers - To plan and track development activities
    d) System Administrators - To understand deployment and operational needs
    e) Stakeholders - To validate business requirements alignment
    f) Maintenance Teams - To understand system behavior for future enhancements

The document defines:
    - Functional requirements with numbered specifications
    - Non-functional requirements including quality attributes
    - System constraints and assumptions
    - External interface requirements
    - Data requirements and entity relationships

1.2 SCOPE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.2.1 Product Name: EasyHire (Recruitment and Interview Management System)

1.2.2 Product Overview:
EasyHire is an enterprise web application that automates the complete hiring 
workflow from job vacancy creation through employee onboarding decision. The 
system provides a centralized platform for HR teams, department heads, and 
interviewers to collaborate on recruitment activities.

1.2.4 Benefits:
    - Streamlined recruitment workflow
    - Reduced manual effort in candidate tracking
    - Improved communication with candidates
    - Data-driven hiring decisions
    - Complete audit trail for compliance
    - Role-based access control for security

1.2.5 Objectives:
    - Provide 360-degree view of recruitment process
    - Enable collaboration between HR and departments
    - Automate routine communication tasks
    - Generate actionable reports for decision-making
    - Ensure data security and access control

1.3 DEFINITIONS, ACRONYMS, AND ABBREVIATIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.3.1 Definitions:

    Candidate       - An individual who applies for a job vacancy
    Vacancy         - An open job position requiring candidates
    Interview       - A scheduled evaluation session with candidate
    Stage           - A numbered phase in multi-interview process
    Review          - Written feedback by interviewer
    Selection Status- Candidate's progress (Received/Viewed/Considering)
    Interview Status- Candidate's interview result (Pending/Passed/Rejected)
    Recall          - Bringing back a previous candidate for new vacancy
    Employment      - Final hiring decision for a candidate
    Template        - Pre-defined email format with placeholders
